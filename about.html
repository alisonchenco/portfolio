Hello, I am Alison 👋🏻

Tech Enthusiast, LEGO Fan, Foodie

Currently, I am a junior @UC San Diego studying Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction. The interdisciplinary nature of cognitive science enables me to speak the language of researchers, designers, and developers, which helps bridge the gap between technology and design.

My leadership experience @Design Co, UCSD’s largest design community, has been fundamental in my growth as a designer. I am grateful to be a part of such a supportive community, where I can learn from my peers and lead workshops to pass on my knowledge to new designers.

As a tech enthusiast, I am excited by the latest technologies and the design opportunities that emerge along with it. I love designing for digital experiences and I believe that technology and design are inseparable. Outside of work and school, I like to spend time building Legos 👾, food hunting 🍣, watching movies 🍿, and reading books 📖 (currently reading Blueprint: The Evolutionary Origins of A Good Society).
