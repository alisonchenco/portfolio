UCSD TASA

TASA, Taiwanese American Student Association, is a social organization that holds social events and promotes cultural awareness by building a community. We strive to create a sense of unity not only within the Taiwanese community here in San Diego, but also with students at other universities.

Logo

UCSD TASA introduces new logo every year. The brand has a different theme of the year based on the designer. During 2017, I designed a new logo. I chose yellow because it is associated with joy, happiness and energy, and arouses cheerfulness.
