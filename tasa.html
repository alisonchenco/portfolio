UCSD TASA

TASA, Taiwanese American Student Association, is a social organization that holds social events and promotes cultural awareness by building a community. We strive to create a sense of unity not only within the Taiwanese community here in San Diego, but also with students at other universities.



Logo

UCSD TASA introduces new logo every year. The brand has a different theme of the year based on the designer. During 2017, I designed a new logo. I chose yellow because it is associated with joy, happiness and energy, and arouses cheerfulness.

Banner

The design of the banner was inspired by Taipei 101, one of the tallest skyscrapers in the world. The prominent architecture is highly identical.



Membership Card

In order to keep the brand identity align, I also used Taipei 101 as an element for the membership card. In the front, it simply shows the brand logo. In the back, it lists out partnered restaurants with a cartoony Taipei 101.

Final Design

Taiwan Black Bear aka. the Formosan black bear is a subspecies of the Asiatic black bear. Formosan black bears are endemic to Taiwan; therefore, I decided to use Taiwan bear as our brand character and placed them into the landscape of Taiwan.



Hoodie

Design

For the winter hoodie, I was inspired by the Yushan, the tallest moutain in Taiwan. Since TASA's tradition is to go skiing in the winter, I designed the major image to be the Yushan mount.
