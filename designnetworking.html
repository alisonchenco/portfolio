Overview

The leadership team of Design at UCSD, the largest pre-professional design thinking organization at UC San Diego, was recently invited to experience the offices of Google, Lyft, and LinkedIn. Board members were able to travel to San Francisco over Presidents’ Day weekend, sponsored by UCSD Design Lab. In previous years, the student group has toured companies such as Adobe, Wish, Salesforce, IDEO, and Workday. This annual trip allows students to engage with professional designers, gaining insight into the logistics of corporate design.
