Reflection

During summer 2018, I was a UX Researcher Intern in Microprogram. In which, the company was developing an advertising platform on LINE Co (the largest communication app from Japan). In which, my role was to focus on understanding the target companies that would be interested in the advertising platform. The goal of the platform is to send geo-targeted advertisement to LINE users. Since many of my work is under NDA, I can only talk about the takeaways I learned during the internship.


UX Research

In the process, we had several interviews with a consultant from a marketing firm to identify the interested customers. In which, I learned to interview in great details. Before interviewing the consultant, we generate the useful question to target the information. During the interview, we record the audio and take notes. As the consultant deviates into unrelated path, we try to control the flow of the conversation back on track. We constantly asked follow up questions when the interviewee touched on something that may be important or interesting. After the interview, we listen to the recording, synthesize and analyze the information received. All in all, I had learned that interview is one of the most critical processes in conducting user research. Discovering more about the market and user is essential before building a successful product and service.
