This summer, I had the opportunity to work as a UX Design intern at BD, for the Technology Solution team (Main Project) and UX Design team (UXD Project).


01. Main Project: RSS Proactive Feature

UX Design + Interaction Design

For the Technology Solution team, my primary role was user experience design and interaction design. Together with other interns (5 engineers and 1 PM), we designed and developed an application that helps hundreds of engineers to better troubleshoot medical devices in a cloud based platform.

Background

BD Remote Support Service (RSS) is the BD enterprise-class solution offering remote management and support of BD products deployed at healthcare organization. Using industry-standard secure technologies, the team of BD RSS technicians ensures customers to receive quick and proactive support. It serves more than 140,000 BD products around the world with 24/7 support.

Design Goal
Process

During the design and development process, our team was introduced with Agile Scrum development framework. Agile Scrum is a software development method that helps team to work efficiently, it encourages teams to learn through experiences, self organize while working on a problem, and reflect on the wins and losses to continuously improve. I found the Agile Scrum framework helpful, which helped me to track the process of the development team.

Visual Design

  • Hardware Product: BD Pyxis Medstation, BD FACSCanto II, BD Max
Draft

After exploring some designs, I decided on isometric design, since it is the latest design trends and it would work well with BD‚Äôs medical devices. Isometric design is the combination of flat design and 3D design, and there are no converging perspective lines. I really like how isometric is simple, yet gives you more perspectives on an object.
